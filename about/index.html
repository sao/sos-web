About

SOS Crane was founded in July 1989 in Signal Hill California by Steve and Kim Whaley primarily to service the oilfield, construction and energy industries in California. The business was expanded in 1995 with the addition of a yard facility in Bakersfield. Further growth of the Company has resulted in a new Company headquarters and 20-acre yard facility with rail access and pipe storage yard on Enos Lane in Bakersfield.

The pride of the company is our fleet of over 35 trucks consisting of 19 Ton Cranes, Winch Trucks and Straight Truck. Our diverse fleet makes SOS the ideal choice for many service needs across the oilfield industry. With 24 hr dispatch 7 days a week, SOS is always available to meet the needs of our customers and ensure logistical efficiency is delivered.

We donate to multiple charities such as Challenge Athlete Fund, MSD Foundation, and multiple local youth sports. In addition, we are active members of the WBNEC and Signal Hill Chamber of Commerce.
