Safety

SOS Crane and Trucking’s primary mission is to create and implement practices that result in SOS being the safest transportation company in California. SOS is committed to the safety and welfare of all, directly and indirectly, involved with SOS. We aim to achieve Incident Free Operation through education and awareness of dangers and risk that are present in the workplace.

SOS goes the extra mile to ensure our employees are trained and equipped with the tools and skills necessary to return home injury free on a daily basis. From Smith’s Defensive Training to and extensive onboarding process, SOS employees are always fit for duty and ready to do the job right the first time.
